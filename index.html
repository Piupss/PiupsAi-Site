Which professions will AI replace?

In the next decade, artificial intelligence is expected to make human participation unnecessary in most productive and intellectual activities, according to Bill Gates. The Microsoft co-founder said in an interview with comedian Jimmy Fallon on NBC's The Tonight Show that recent advances have made it possible to imagine a scenario in which "excellent medical advice and great tutoring" are freely available.

Brazil is the main AI hub in Latin America!
